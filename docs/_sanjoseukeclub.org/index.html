All skill levels are welcome

Where: Denny's Restaurant, 1140 Hillsdale Avenue, San Jose.

When: the FIRST WEDNESDAY of the month

Time: We offer a free, half-hour beginner lesson starting at 6:30 pm.  Click here for the four lesson syllabus and  here for the chord charts,

 The general meeting begins at 7, with a 10-15 minute break at 8. Meeting ends at 9 pm.

We have the meeting room  from 5 until 9 p.m. which is ample time to have dinner, chat with fellow members, and practice before the meeting.

  It is a good idea to arrive as early as possible.

We project the song sheets so paper song sheets are not needed.
